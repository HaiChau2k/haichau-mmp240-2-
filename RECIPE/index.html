Best Egg Tarts Recipe

Want a different dessert for your meal? This guide will show you how to make perfect egg tarts for dessert or an afternoon tea party.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter, chilled
  • 2 tablespoons cold water

For the custard filling:

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Make the dough: Mix flour and salt. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add water and form into a ball. Chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling: Dissolve sugar in hot water. Let cool, then add evaporated milk, eggs, and vanilla. Strain the mixture.
  3. Assemble tarts: Roll out dough and cut circles to line tart molds. Pour filling into each shell (3/4 full).
  4. Bake: Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes until filling is set and crust is golden.
  5. Cool: Let cool in pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
